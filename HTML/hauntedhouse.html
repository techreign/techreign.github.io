The Manor of the Late Widow

I am not the best at telling stories, but this one is quite near and dear to me. There was once a lady who lived with her husband in a large manor near the corner of a small village. They loved eachother dearly, and spent all their free time happily together.
But one day, something changed. The husband became a lot more distant and would randomly start fights with the poor old lady. Soon, she discovered that he was cheating on her with another woman from the village.
She killed her husband by stabbing a knife through his heart, and not long after did the same to herself. A rather similar but more tragic Romeo and Juliet, I would say.
Rumour had it that her ghosts still haunts the house. A brave little kid named Jim wanted to see if this was true, as he did not believe in ghosts.
Jim approached the "haunted" house and to his surprise he saw a shadow figure from the window.
He quickly hid behind some bushes.
Then... all of a sudden, the widow's ghost came out and grabbed him! AND STABBED HIM IN THE HEART!


The end...
